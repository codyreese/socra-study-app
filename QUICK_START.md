## Study Tips

### Week 1-2: Foundation
1. Go through all flashcards 2-3 times
2. Read the entire study guide
3. Take your first practice test to see where you stand

### Week 3-4: Practice
1. Take domain-specific practice tests
2. Focus on your weak areas (Analytics will show you)
3. Use the AI Tutor to ask about confusing topics
4. Review flashcards daily

### Week 5-6: Exam Ready
1. Take full practice exams
2. Aim for 85%+ consistently
3. Review any remaining weak areas
4. Stay confident - you've got this!

## The Real Exam

**What to expect:**
- 130 questions total (100 count toward your score)
- Need 71% to pass (71 out of 100)
- Computer-based at Prometric center
- Get results immediately
- Bring two forms of ID

**Content breakdown:**
- 40% Study Start-Up (protocol, IRB, consent)
- 50% Study Implementation (GCP, monitoring, AEs)
- 10% Study Closure (archiving, final reports)
